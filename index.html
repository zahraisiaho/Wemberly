ABOUT US.

We believe life is all about living, hence our motto, EAT, DRINK, DON’T THINK at Wemberly.

Leafy green surroundings, a cool breeze, great food, and a relaxed ambience makes Wemberly stands out as one of the rare restaurants that allows you to immerse yourself in the best experience of al fresco dining within the city in a lush, sunken, multi-level garden setting with secret nooks and hidden crannies. In the evenings, fairy lights, candles, and charcoal braziers set the tone for a warm and romantic atmosphere with a cozy indoor dining option complete with a wood-burning fireplace for the colder months.

Why Us

Why Choose Our Restaurant

01

Our Ambience

Wemberly is one of the most exquisite fine-dinning restaurant in twin cities with a captivating view of Murree Hills, perfect ambiance and scrumptious food. Our team is always looking forward to provide you exceptional services and win your hearts out.

02

Dine

Meals at Wemberly are made onsite from scratch. This includes all the spring rolls, curry puffs, roti and desserts. By creating everything from the start, we have 100% control over the ingredients used, the quality and the freshness of the dish. You will taste the difference.

03

Location

We are located along Riverside Drive which is in Riverside in Nairobi County, about a fifteen-minute drive to Nairobi CBD.
